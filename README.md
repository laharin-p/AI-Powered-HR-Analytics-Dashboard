# 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges organizations face today. Losing skilled employees increases hiring costs, reduces productivity, and affects business growth.

This project combines **Machine Learning, Python, and Power BI** to build an **AI-powered HR Analytics solution** that not only analyzes employee data but also predicts employees who are at high risk of leaving the organization.

Instead of simply reporting historical HR metrics, this solution delivers **predictive insights**, **AI-driven recommendations**, and **interactive dashboards** that help HR teams make proactive, data-driven decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to answer critical questions such as:

- Which employees are most likely to leave?
- Which departments have the highest attrition?
- Does overtime influence employee turnover?
- How do salary, experience, and job satisfaction impact attrition?
- What actions should HR take to improve employee retention?

This project addresses these questions using predictive analytics and business intelligence.

# 📈 Key Business Insights

- Research & Development and Sales departments show higher employee attrition.

- Employees working overtime are significantly more likely to leave.

- Lower job satisfaction is strongly associated with higher attrition.

- Employees with lower monthly income demonstrate increased resignation risk.

- AI successfully identifies employees requiring proactive HR intervention.
